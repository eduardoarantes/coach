The **Recommendations** page delivers AI guidance on what to do today — and how to optimize your week. It analyzes recovery data, training load, and your planned workouts to suggest whether to proceed, modify, or rest.

## Today's recommendation

Each day, the AI evaluates:

- **Today's planned workout** — duration, intensity, TSS, structure
- **Recovery state** — HRV, sleep quality, resting heart rate
- **Recent training** — last 7 days of load, CTL/ATL trends
- **Athlete profile** — FTP, goals, historical recovery patterns

The recommendation is one of:

| Action               | Meaning                                  |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Proceed**          | Recovery supports the planned session    |
| **Modify**           | Adjust intensity, duration, or structure |
| **Reduce intensity** | Keep the session but make it easier      |
| **Rest**             | Take a recovery day                      |

Each recommendation includes **reasoning** — the specific factors that drove the decision (e.g., "HRV 15% below baseline, poor sleep").

![Today's Training recommendation card showing a rest-day decision, the reasoning behind it, and controls to view details or refresh](/media/docs/daily-training-card.jpg)

Use the recommendation card as a decision aid: read the explanation first, then open the details when you need the supporting context.

## Weekly recommendations

Beyond daily guidance, the AI reviews your week for:

- Load distribution imbalances
- Missing key session types (long ride, intervals, recovery)
- Recovery patterns suggesting overreaching
- Alignment with your active [training plan](/documentation/athletes/training-plans)

## Using recommendations

1. Open **Recommendations** from the sidebar
2. Review the **Focus Area** — pinned high-priority recommendations
3. Read the reasoning before accepting or dismissing
4. Click **Update Recommendations** to regenerate with the latest data

### Accepting or dismissing

- **Accept** — marks the recommendation as actioned; may trigger plan changes
- **Dismiss** — removes it from your active list
- **Pin** — keeps important recommendations visible in Focus Area

## Chat integration

Ask your coach to explain or act on recommendations:

- _"Why does today's recommendation say rest?"_
- _"Accept the modified workout suggestion."_
- _"What should I change about this week's plan?"_

## Dashboard widget

Your Dashboard surfaces the most urgent daily recommendation so you do not need to navigate to the full page every morning. Pair it with [Morning Check-in](/documentation/athletes/dashboard-daily-flow) for the complete daily workflow.
