[Wahoo](https://www.wahoofitness.com) devices — including ELEMNT bike computers and TICKR heart rate monitors — sync through **Wahoo Cloud**.

## Setup Guide

1. Go to **Settings → Apps** on the Dashboard.
2. Click **Connect with Wahoo**.
3. Authorize Coach Watts to access your Wahoo Cloud account.

## What syncs?

- **Activities** — rides and runs recorded on Wahoo devices
- **Heart rate data** — from TICKR and built-in sensors
- **Power data** — from connected power meters via Wahoo head units
- **GPS routes** — map data for outdoor activities

## Tips

- Wahoo activities also appear on Strava if you have Strava sync enabled on Wahoo. Coach Watts deduplicates overlapping activities — connect only one primary activity source to avoid confusion.
- For indoor rides on Wahoo KICKR, ensure the activity is saved to Wahoo Cloud (not just the training app) for sync.

## Troubleshooting

- **No activities appearing** — verify the ride was uploaded to Wahoo Cloud, then click **Sync** on the Dashboard
- **Missing power data** — confirm your power meter was connected during the recording
- **Duplicate workouts** — if Strava is also connected, Coach Watts may merge duplicates automatically
