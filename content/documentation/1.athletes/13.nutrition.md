Coach Watts goes beyond calorie counting with **dynamic periodized fueling** — your daily targets adapt to workout intensity, training phase, and personal sensitivity.

For the science behind calculations, see [Nutrition & Fueling Logic](/documentation/athletes/fueling-logic). This guide covers how to use nutrition features day to day.

## Daily nutrition view

Open **Nutrition** from the sidebar. Each day shows:

- **Target calories and macros** — adjusted for that day's training load
- **Fueling windows** — PRE, INTRA, and POST workout timing
- **Logged meals** — entries from manual logging or [Yazio](/documentation/athletes/integrations/yazio) sync
- **Adherence scores** — how closely intake matches targets

Navigate between days using the date picker or sidebar shortcuts (Today, Tomorrow, Yesterday).

![Nutrition Profile card summarizing nutrition pattern, caloric balance, macro distribution, and key observations](/media/docs/nutrition-profile.jpg)

The profile summary turns your logged intake into a short, actionable picture of the habits most likely to affect recovery.

## How daily targets are set

Your targets combine:

1. **Basal metabolic rate** — calculated from age, weight, height, and sex
2. **Activity level** — NEAT multiplier for daily movement
3. **Workout fuel state** — Eco, Steady, or Performance based on session intensity
4. **Training phase** — Base, Build, or Race presets
5. **Fueling sensitivity** — your personal carb scaling factor

Workout days add structured fueling windows. Rest days maintain baseline targets without workout-specific windows.

## Logging food

### Manual entry

Add meals directly on the nutrition page:

1. Select a meal slot (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack)
2. Enter calories, protein, carbs, and fat
3. Save — adherence scores update immediately

### Yazio sync

Connect [Yazio](/documentation/athletes/integrations/yazio) to import logged meals automatically. Coach Watts maps Yazio entries to the correct day and meal slot.

## Fueling windows

On training days, nutrition is structured around your workout:

| Window    | Purpose                                       |
| --------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| **PRE**   | Prime glycogen before the session             |
| **INTRA** | Sustain energy during efforts over 90 minutes |
| **POST**  | Recovery and muscle protein synthesis         |

The AI coach can suggest specific meals for each window. Ask in Chat:

- _"What should I eat before tomorrow's long ride?"_
- _"Give me a post-workout meal with 40g protein."_

## Nutrition analysis scores

The AI scores your daily nutrition on:

- **Overall** — holistic daily quality
- **Macro balance** — protein, carb, and fat distribution
- **Quality** — food choices relative to training demands
- **Adherence** — how closely you hit targets
- **Hydration** — fluid intake relative to sweat loss estimates

## Settings

Configure nutrition behavior in **Profile → Settings → Nutrition**:

- Fueling sensitivity multiplier
- Training phase preset
- Fuel state intensity thresholds
- Weight goal (lose, maintain, gain)
