## AI Coaching

Our core engine analyzes your historical data to provide real-time feedback and session adjustments.

- **Personalized feedback** — after every workout, the AI evaluates performance relative to your target zones
- **Dynamic adjustments** — if you miss a session or overreach, the coach updates your training plan
- **Daily recommendations** — proceed, modify, or rest based on recovery and load
- **Background execution** — complex plan generation runs asynchronously with notifications

## Training Plans

Build goal-driven training blocks for cycling, running, or triathlon.

- **Block periodization** — base, build, peak, and taper phases structured around your event
- **Plan wizard** — set goals, availability, and strategy before generating
- **Adaptation** — push forward, recalculate, or mark sick when life intervenes
- **Workout matching** — link completed activities to planned sessions
