Welcome to the Coach Watts developer documentation. This section is designed for integration partners and developers looking to extend the platform or integrate with our API.

## Getting started

1. **[Developer Portal](/documentation/developers/developer-portal)** — register your OAuth application
2. **[Authentication](/documentation/developers/authentication)** — OAuth 2.0 with PKCE
3. **[Scopes](/documentation/developers/scopes)** — permission reference
4. **[Syncing Data](/documentation/developers/syncing-data)** — upload workouts, wellness, and nutrition
