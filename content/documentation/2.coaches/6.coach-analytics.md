**Coaching → Analytics** provides squad-level insights beyond what you see on individual athlete dashboards.

## What coach analytics covers

- **Training load trends** — CTL/ATL progression across your roster
- **Compliance rates** — planned vs. completed workout ratios
- **Recovery patterns** — athletes consistently under-recovered or overreaching
- **Performance comparisons** — relative fitness development within a squad

## Using analytics as a coach

### Weekly squad review

1. Open **Coaching → Analytics**
2. Review load distribution across athletes
3. Identify athletes who need plan adjustments
4. [Act As](/documentation/coaches/acting-as-athlete) specific athletes to drill into details

### Before check-ins

Pull analytics before scheduled athlete conversations:

- Has compliance dropped in the last two weeks?
- Is anyone's fatigue (ATL) spiking while fitness (CTL) stalls?
- Are recovery scores trending down across the squad?

### Team-level view

For [team](/documentation/coaches/team-management) coaches, analytics can highlight group trends — useful before camps, race blocks, or recovery weeks.

## Combining analytics with AI

While acting as an athlete, use **Chat** to investigate analytics findings:

- _"Why has my compliance dropped this month?"_
- _"Compare my training load to the last block."_
- _"Am I overreaching based on recent trends?"_

You see the same AI analysis the athlete would, plus your coaching perspective.
