Coach Watts supports **teams** for coaches managing groups of athletes — clubs, training squads, or multi-coach organizations.

## Teams vs. individual coaching

| Feature          | Individual coaching    | Teams                          |
| ---------------- | ---------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Connect athletes | Invite code (1:1)      | Team roster with shared access |
| Calendar         | Per-athlete view       | Team-wide scheduling           |
| Plans & library  | Per-athlete assignment | Share across team members      |
| Access control   | Coach ↔ athlete        | Coach ↔ team ↔ athletes        |

## Creating a team

1. Navigate to **Coaching → Team**
2. Click **Create Team**
3. Enter a team name and description
4. Your team appears in the team list

## Inviting members

### Invite coaches or staff

1. Open your team page
2. Go to **Invites**
3. Generate an invite link or code
4. Share with assistant coaches or staff members

### Add athletes to a team

Athletes can join via:

- **Team invite code** — athlete enters the code on the join page
- **Coach adds directly** — from the team roster management page

## Team roster

The roster page shows all team members with:

- Name and sport profiles
- Connection status
- Quick links to [Act As](/documentation/coaches/acting-as-athlete) and [Calendar](/documentation/coaches/coaching-calendar)

## Managing a team

- **Edit team details** — name, description from team settings
- **Remove members** — revoke an athlete's team access
- **Delete team** — removes the team; individual coach-athlete connections may remain
