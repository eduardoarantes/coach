The **Coaching Calendar** gives you a bird's-eye view of your athletes' schedules — planned workouts, completed sessions, and rest days across your entire roster.

## Opening the calendar

Navigate to **Coaching → Calendar** from the sidebar.

## Calendar views

- **Single athlete** — select an athlete to see their full training calendar
- **Multi-athlete** — compare schedules across your roster (availability-dependent)

The calendar shows both **planned workouts** from training plans and **completed activities** synced from integrations.

## Acting on calendar data

From the calendar you can:

1. Click a workout to view details
2. [Act As](/documentation/coaches/acting-as-athlete) the athlete to make changes
3. Open the athlete's [training plan](/documentation/athletes/training-plans) for block-level adjustments
4. Use **Chat** while acting as the athlete to modify sessions

## Scheduling workflows

Common coach workflows:

- **Weekly review** — scan the upcoming week for load distribution across athletes
- **Conflict detection** — spot athletes with back-to-back hard sessions before a race
- **Compliance check** — compare planned vs. completed sessions at a glance

## Team calendar

If you manage a [team](/documentation/coaches/team-management), the calendar can show team members' schedules together. Useful for group rides, shared rest days, or camp planning.
